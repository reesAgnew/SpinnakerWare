Mission

Deliver a simple, affordable, and comprehensive solution for today's Optometric practices.

History

Beginning in Ontario and expanding across the country, OD Office Pro has been delivering solutions for optometrists since 2006. With over 250 offices installed, and a continued expansion across the country, we have established ourselves as a presence in the office management space.

Consulting with practicing Optometrists and their staff, SpinnakerWare Inc. has developed OD Office Pro with the intent to increase your staff's satisfaction by addressing specific scheduling and billing requirements.

Our team of professional software developers have worked and continue to work closely with a number of optometrists and their staff to define the latest in office management solutions.
